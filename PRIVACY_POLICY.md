# Privacy Policy for NovelNest

**Effective Date:** April 24, 2026

**NovelNest** ("we," "our," or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our application.

### 1. Information Collection and Use
NovelNest is designed as a local-first application. 
* **Personal Data:** We do not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information (such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers).
* **Usage Data:** The App does not transmit your reading habits, library contents, or search history to any external servers owned by the developer.

### 2. Permissions
To provide its core functionality, NovelNest may request the following permissions:
* **Storage/Media Access:** Required to read novel files stored on your device and to save your reading progress locally.
* **Internet Access:** Required if you choose to fetch novel metadata or content from third-party sources/extensions.

### 3. Third-Party Services & Extensions
NovelNest may allow the use of third-party extensions to access external content. Please be aware that:
* We do not control the privacy practices of these third-party sources.
* Interacting with external sources may subject you to their respective privacy policies.

### 4. Data Security
Because your data is stored locally on your device, the security of your data depends on your device's security settings. We do not have access to your local files or reading database.

### 5. Children’s Privacy
Our App does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us.

### 6. Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
